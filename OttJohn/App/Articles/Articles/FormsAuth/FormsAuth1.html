While creating this site for the fun of it, I am embracing the opportunity to review a lot of the concepts that went into building some of my other, prefessional sites. When it comes to membership, I have elected to use ASP.NET Membership Services together with Forms Authentication. This really is not too difficult to set up, but if you are hurrying through the process, there are definitely a couple of gotcha's waiting for you.

With respect to how this article is going to go down, I will talk about installing ASP.NET Membership Services first, then configuring forms authentication (which will work with a JSON client), then adding functionality that will allow us to retrieve user roles and stuff them into the HttpContext object.
