He yawned and returned the documents to his briefcase, then took out his PDA and typed in a personal reminder to continue with the marketing document when he had the time. Perhaps in the car on the way to the interview with Sanchez.

When a mail message is retrieved by the browser, its content is simply presented as web page. Because of the way email is stored in these kinds of systems, those who choose to use them must be very careful about what they say.
